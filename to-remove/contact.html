Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our service.

How quickly do you respond to inquiries?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters related to upcoming deliveries, please call our customer service line for immediate assistance.

Do you offer corporate or event rentals?

Yes! We provide kitchenware for corporate retreats, team-building cooking events, and private gatherings. Contact our events team at events@kitchenlux.com for custom quotes and special arrangements.

Can I request delivery to a location not listed in your service areas?

We're constantly expanding our service areas. For locations not currently listed, contact us at least 2 weeks in advance, and we'll do our best to accommodate your request if logistically possible.
